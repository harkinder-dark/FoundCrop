About

  • Found-Crop is B2B agricultural palteforme that help to connect agricultural supply and demand.In Togo for example it is difficult to find certain agricultural products at a certain time of the year. Research methods are still word of mouth. Coming from a farming family. After the harvest, our only way to sell our products is to let those around us know. Sometimes we are forced to sell the harvest at a low price, for lack of choice. It is in this momentum that for this Portfolio project at the end of training at Holberton School, we decided to create this platform
